Professional Development

Requesting Letters of Recommendation

Send one email with:
1) Name + CUNY ID + course(s)/term(s) + final grade(s)
2) Deadline(s) & submission method (portal/email)
3) CV/Resume + unofficial transcript
4) What you’re applying for & what you want me to emphasize in my letter
5) Required forms (PDF) & FERPA waiver status, if needed
6) ≥ 3 weeks’ notice before first deadline
